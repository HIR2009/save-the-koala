What Does A Koala Like

Koalas eat mainly eucalyptus leaves (gum leaves). Occasionally they eat the leaves from some other native Australian trees, and they also use certain trees just for resting in. Koalas live in tall open eucalypt (gum tree) forests. The areas of bushland where they like to live are called their HABITATS.
